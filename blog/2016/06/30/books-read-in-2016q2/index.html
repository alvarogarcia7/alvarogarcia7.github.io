Books read in 2016Q2

Jun 30, 2016 - 1 minute read - Comments - bookreading2016Q2self-studybuilding-microservicesrelease-it

Books I’ve read this quarter1:

  • Building Microservices, Designing Fine-Grained Systems, by Sam Newman, with the ‘@deAprendices’s reading club’. Blog posts under the tag building-microservices

Books started, not yet finished (WIP):

  • Domain-Driven Design: Tackling Complexity in the Heart of Software by Eric Evans, again.
  • Amazon Web Services in Action by Michael Wittig and Andreas Wittig
  • Reinventing Organizations by Frederic Laloux. Blog posts under the tag reinventing-organizations
  • Extreme Programming Explained: Embrace Change, 2nd Edition by Kent Beck and Cynthia Andres, again.
  • Release It! - Design and Deploy Production-Ready Software, by Michael T. Nygard, with the ‘@deAprendices’s reading club’. Blog posts under the tag release-it

Books I want to finish. Usually come from another quarter:

  • Why programmers work at night by Swizec Teller
  • Readings in Database Systems, 5th Edition by Peter Bailis, Joseph M. Hellerstein, Michael Stonebraker, editors

Books that have entered the queue:

  • Serverless - Patterns of Modern Application Design Using Microservices (Amazon Web Services Edition) by Obie Fernandez
